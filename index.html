Why Us

Science Meets AI

Founded by a researcher with a PhD in Microbiology & Infectious Diseases, an MS in Biomedical Informatics, and an MS in Biomedical Sciences. After 12+ years at the bench at MD Anderson and UTHealth, the frustration was clear: too much time spent on manual data entry, paper logs, and repetitive tasks instead of actual science. Pipetting with gloves on while trying to type notes into a spreadsheet. Losing observations because there was no easy way to capture them in the moment.

JoeLuT AI was built to fix that. We build AI tools that help researchers focus on the science, not the paperwork. And because we also run a food manufacturing business, we bring that same hands-on approach to food manufacturers who are drowning in manual processes.
